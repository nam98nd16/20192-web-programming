5 kinds of indentifiers

1. Student Number: used in schools to identify students

2. Căn cước công dân: used in Vietnam to verify the citizenship of a person

3. Driver license: used to specify a person to be able to drive or operate a specific type of vehicle

4. Birth certificate: A birth certificate is a vital record that documents the birth of a child

5. Family register: a civil registry used in many countries to track information of a genealogical or family-centric legal interest
